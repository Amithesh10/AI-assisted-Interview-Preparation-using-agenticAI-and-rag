Common HR interview questions:
1. Tell me about yourself.
2. Why should we hire you?
3. What are your strengths and weaknesses?
4. Where do you see yourself in 5 years?
5. Explain a challenging situation you handled.

For technical interviews, candidates should prepare role-specific questions based on the job description.

For data roles, important topics include SQL, Power BI, Python, Machine Learning, data cleaning, dashboards, and business insights.
